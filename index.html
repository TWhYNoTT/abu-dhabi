About The event

The Statistics Centre - Abu Dhabi (SCAD) organises Abu Dhabi Statistics Stakeholders Forum to enhance collaboration between government entities and the private sector in developing Abu Dhabi’s statistical ecosystem. The Forum focuses on the topic of digital transformation within the realm of statistics and the development of Abu Dhabi’s decentralised statistical ecosystem.

The forum discusses enhancing the government statistical capabilities with the aim of achieving statistical maturity. Additionally, it emphasises the importance of data collaboration to enhance decision-making processes based on reliable and high-quality statistical indicators. During the forum, SCAD will pay tribute to its strategic stakeholders and field researchers, while also providing an overview of SCAD’s significant accomplishments through its collaboration with strategic stakeholders.

PANEL DISCUSSIONS

The forum will feature insightful panel discussions with government entities and private sector companies, focusing on the role of administrative data and data collaboration to promote economic and social growth in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi.

Government Sector

The panel discussions with government entities cover the role of admin data in transforming Abu Dhabi’s social and economic aspirations.

Private Sector

The panel discussions with private sector companies cover enhancing the B2G data collaboration for public good in Abu Dhabi.
